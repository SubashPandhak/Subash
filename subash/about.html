About Us


We have been one of Nepal’s leading hospitality brands since 1992.Roadhouse has been committed to maintaining the highquality and standards that our customers have been accustomed that invokes quality service, food as well as its uniqueambiance that has been well received over the year.The brand initially started with the two Kayestha brothers opening Roadhouse Café at the heart of the city in Thamel,inspired by the hit song-“Roadhouse Blues” by The Doors.Over the years the Roadhouse Group now claims five Roadhouse Cafes, Roadhouse Pizzeria, the swanky Mezze by Roadhouse,classic Italian restaurant - La Dolce Vita, a resort in Pokhara called Temple Tree and as the hospitality groupcontinues to grow, Hotel Roadhouse has been recently included to the list of properties with its opening in the year 2018.
